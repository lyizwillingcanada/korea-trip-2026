🚕 Taxis (supplemental)
Available but expensive for cross-island trips

Use for short hops only · 3 cabs needed for group of 12
🗺 Key distances from Jeju City
Hallasan trailhead → ~30 min drive

Manjanggul Lava Tube → ~45 min drive northeast
Hyeopjae Beach → ~30 min drive west
💡 Recommended approach by city
Seoul — Use T-Money subway daily (~$15/pp/day). Split into 2–3 taxis for night outings (~$5–8/pp per trip). Hire a van only for the airport run or day trips out of the city.
Busan — Subway for Haeundae & central spots. Charter a van for Gamcheon, Haedong, and Taejongdae — 1 day van ~$300–400 CAD covers those all in one shot.
Jeju — Hire a driver + van for all 4 days. ~$400–500 CAD/day split 12 ways = ~$33–42/person/day. Easiest, no navigation stress, driver knows the island.
